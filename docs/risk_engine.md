# Risk Engine

## Future Role
- Pre-trade risk controls
- Kill switch for emergency stop
- Exposure limits per user/portfolio
- Integrates with execution and portfolio engines

## Pre-Trade Risk Controls
- Validate order size, exposure, and compliance before execution

## Kill Switch
- Emergency stop for all trading activity

## Exposure Limits
- Per-user and per-portfolio limits

## Relation to Portfolio/Execution
- Risk checks before order is sent to execution engine
